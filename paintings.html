Pumpkinferno

Famous Paintings

Pumpkinferno is an event that uses over 7000 artificial pumpkins to create displays up to 16 feet tall.

This exhibet is made up of famous portraits re-created in 12 foot tall pumpkin displays.

My role in the event was designing the illustrations that get put on the pumpkins and carved. For this exhibet, I hand painted the backgrounds to look like the original paintings.
