Pumpkinferno

Jailhouse Rock

Pumpkinferno is an event that uses over 7000 artificial pumpkins to create displays up to 16 feet tall.

The Jailhouse Rock exhibet took famous icons and put them in Elvis's Jailhouse Rock environment.

My role in the event was designing the illustrations that get put on the pumpkins and carved.
